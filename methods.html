Methods

On the left, stands Colored Integration. As I wanted to work with buildings and explore architecture, the project started as two separate sketches of different structures. A shopping mall and a wooden staircase library, placed on the upper part and lower part of the drawing respectively. The integration was done by pens and markers. In the middle is a corridor painted with ink and water. The objective for this project was to use ink to sketch a scene, my choice being an empty hallway. On the right is a colored pencil drawing of Turkish breakfast, my favorite meal of the day. I decided to portray something I love about my culture and also improve my coloring skills.
